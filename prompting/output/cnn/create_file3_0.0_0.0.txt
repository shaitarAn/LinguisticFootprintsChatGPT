(CNN) -- A massive fire broke out at a popular amusement park in Greenford, Ohio, on Saturday, causing extensive damage to several attractions and leaving dozens of visitors injured. The incident occurred at approximately 3:00 p.m. local time at the Greenford Adventure Park, a beloved family destination known for its thrilling rides and entertainment.

Eyewitnesses reported seeing thick plumes of smoke and flames engulfing the park's iconic Ferris wheel, which quickly spread to nearby structures. Firefighters from multiple departments rushed to the scene to battle the blaze, working tirelessly to contain the inferno and ensure the safety of park visitors and staff.

According to local authorities, preliminary investigations suggest that the fire may have originated from a malfunction in one of the park's electrical systems. However, a thorough investigation is underway to determine the exact cause of the incident.

"We are deeply saddened by the unfortunate events that unfolded today at Greenford Adventure Park," said park spokesperson, Sarah Thompson. "Our primary concern right now is the well-being of our visitors and employees. We are working closely with the authorities to investigate the incident and provide support to those affected."

Emergency medical teams were dispatched to the scene to treat the injured, many of whom suffered from smoke inhalation and minor burns. The injured individuals were swiftly transported to nearby hospitals, including St. Elizabeth Health Center, where they are receiving medical attention.

Local resident, Mark Johnson, who was at the park with his family when the fire broke out, described the chaotic scene. "It was terrifying. People were running in all directions, trying to find their loved ones and escape the flames. The park staff did their best to guide us to safety, but it was a truly harrowing experience."

Greenford Adventure Park, a staple in the community for over three decades, has been a popular destination for families seeking entertainment and thrills. The park features a variety of rides, including roller coasters, water slides, and a petting zoo. It also hosts live shows and events throughout the year.

The fire has dealt a significant blow to the local economy, as the park attracts thousands of visitors each year, contributing to the tourism industry in Greenford. Local businesses that rely on the park's foot traffic are now concerned about the potential impact on their livelihoods.

Greenford Mayor, Emily Davis, expressed her condolences to those affected by the incident and pledged support to the park's management during this challenging time. "Greenford Adventure Park has been an integral part of our community for many years. We will do everything in our power to assist in the recovery efforts and help the park rebuild."

As investigations into the cause of the fire continue, authorities have temporarily closed the park to ensure the safety of the public. The park's management is working closely with fire officials and safety experts to assess the damage and develop a plan for restoration.

Visitors who had planned to visit the park in the coming days have been advised to seek alternative entertainment options while the park remains closed. The community has rallied together, offering support and assistance to those affected by the devastating incident.

The Greenford Adventure Park fire serves as a stark reminder of the importance of safety measures and regular inspections in public spaces. As the investigation unfolds, authorities will be looking into whether all necessary precautions were in place to prevent such a catastrophic event.
